SourceView Bible

The original four-color Bible experience

SourceView Bible is not just a digital version of a printed Bible. It is a fresh, innovative way to take a deeper look at God's story: what's been written, who said what, and how its words impact and shape our lives, our work, and our world.

Built on over 40,000 hours of original research, SourceView introduced the first major formatting innovation of the biblical text since the introduction of verses during the Reformation — nearly five hundred years ago.

Four-Color Dramatic Text

Red for God, green for lead characters, blue for supporting sources, black for the narrator. Reads like a movie script.

Source Profiles & Metadata

Tap any speaker's name to see all their conversations across every book. Explore 1,400+ indexed biblical sources.

Data Discovery Center

Create pie charts, bar charts, and word clouds. Ask questions never possible before — who speaks most? What words does God say to women?

Seamless Reading

Scroll through entire books without page breaks. Hide chapter and verse numbers. Calculated read times for every book and chapter.

Perfect For

Small groups reading dramatically with each person taking a color. Families wanting to engage children in Scripture. Seminary students exploring source data. Individual study sessions focused on discovering who speaks.

SphereView

Scripture through the lens of seven spheres of influence

Society is shaped by seven spheres of influence. SphereView reveals how Scripture addresses each one through 416 curated key passages — 52 per sphere plus 52 foundational — drawn from all 66 books of the Bible.

Each passage begins with how God seeks to reveal himself through that sphere, then highlights the biblical beliefs, values, principles, and practices appropriate for that area of life.

Religion

God's design for worship, spiritual life, and the community of faith across Scripture.

Family

Biblical principles for marriage, parenting, and household relationships.

Education

How Scripture shapes teaching, learning, discipleship, and the pursuit of knowledge.

Government

God's principles for justice, leadership, law, and the ordering of society.

Media / Communication

The role of language, storytelling, truth-telling, and information in God's design.

Arts & Celebration

Creativity, beauty, music, and celebration as expressions of God's character.

Economy

Biblical wisdom for work, stewardship, commerce, and resource management.

Foundation

52 foundational key passages detailing the basic premises of a Biblical Christian Worldview relevant to every sphere.

VerbView

Seeing God's character in action

VerbView reveals the dynamic, action-driven nature of Scripture by highlighting every verb and action throughout the biblical narrative. See God's character not just in what He says, but in what He does.

Verb Highlighting

Every action word in Scripture made visible — creating a new layer of understanding over the familiar text.

Narrative Flow Analysis

See the movement and momentum of God's story through the verbs that drive each narrative forward.

Character Action Maps

Explore what each biblical character does, not just what they say. Compare the actions of God, prophets, kings, and disciples.

Cross-Sphere Integration

Connect verb patterns with SphereView to see how biblical actions relate to the seven spheres of society.

CommandView

Every instruction God gives, mapped and connected

CommandView maps every instruction, imperative, and command in Scripture — revealing what God calls His people to do. From the Ten Commandments to the Great Commission, see the full scope of God's directives.

Imperative Mapping

Every command in Scripture identified, categorized, and connected to its source, audience, and context.

Speaker Cross-Reference

See which commands come from God directly, from Jesus, from prophets, and from apostles — across the full biblical canon.

Sphere Integration

Connect commands to the seven spheres of society and discover how God's instructions shape every area of life.

Heart-Centered Design

The CommandView icon — tablets with a heart — reflects that God's commands flow from love and lead to abundant life.
